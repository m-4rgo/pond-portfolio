Good to Know

My commissioned work can take a very long time to complete. Depending on the piece, it may take up to several months. I also don't work through my owed list linearly- work that is "ahead" or "behind" in the queue may be completed later or earlier.

I don't really like taking formal commissions! Making work for others is much less about the money for me than it is about having a good time creating a piece of artwork, collaborating on an idea, or having fun with a concept. I am open to requests! If you have an idea, feel free to share it with me. If I get inspired I may make a version of your idea, but I can't promise that I will due to time and mental load limits. Irrespective, I love talking about creative work with people and I love to hear about others' creativity, so don't be afraid to share!

If you want to support me or my work, I do have a ko-fi available, but can't link it for chickensmoothie policy reasons :)
